# Hook Formula Library — @findocere

## Why This File Matters

The hook determines 80% of a post's performance. A perfect post with a dead hook = zero reach. An average post with a killer hook = thousands of views. Always select a hook BEFORE writing the body.

Reference: [[voice/brand-voice]] for tone calibration. [[platforms/x]] for character limits.

## The 3 Psychological Triggers (every hook must hit at least one)

1. **Confusion** — "Wait… what does that mean?" → forces the brain to stop and process
2. **Fear** — "Am I about to miss something?" → activates loss aversion
3. **Desire** — "I want that outcome." → aspirational pull

## 8 Hook Formulas

### H1 — The Confession Hook
**Trigger:** Vulnerability + specificity → trust + curiosity
**Template:** `I [did/believed X]. It cost me [specific consequence].`
**Example:** "I held my DLMM position through a 20% SOL drop. Here's what it actually cost me."
**Best for:** Single tweets, journal posts
**Engagement target:** Replies (people share their own losses)
**Psychological trigger:** Desire (to learn from someone's mistake) + Confusion (why would they admit this?)

### H2 — The Counterintuitive Claim
**Trigger:** Pattern interruption → confusion → scroll stop
**Template:** `[Common belief] is wrong. Here's what actually happens.`
**Example:** "Higher APY doesn't mean higher profit. Most DLMM farmers lose money on the best-looking pools."
**Best for:** Threads (claim → proof → framework)
**Engagement target:** Bookmarks + quote tweets
**Psychological trigger:** Confusion (contradicts what I believed)

### H3 — The Timestamp Hook
**Trigger:** Urgency + freshness → FOMO + relevance
**Template:** `[Time marker]: [unexpected observation from real data].`
**Example:** "Last 72 hours: stablecoin LP yields on Meteora quietly doubled. Nobody's talking about why."
**Best for:** Single tweets, alpha drops
**Engagement target:** Early engagement velocity
**Psychological trigger:** Fear (am I missing this?)

### H4 — The Builder's Receipts
**Trigger:** Proof + aspiration → credibility + desire
**Template:** `[Specific result] from [specific action] in [specific timeframe].`
**Example:** "+6.2% in 11 days on a stablecoin pair. No leverage, no degen plays. Just bin management."
**Best for:** Single tweets with screenshot
**Engagement target:** Quote tweets
**Psychological trigger:** Desire (I want that result)

### H5 — The "Everyone's Ignoring This" Frame
**Trigger:** Exclusivity + implied edge → curiosity + save impulse
**Template:** `Nobody is paying attention to [specific mechanic/data point]. That's the opportunity.`
**Example:** "Nobody is watching Meteora's DAMM V2 fee structure. The edge is in the bin spacing."
**Best for:** Threads
**Engagement target:** Bookmarks + reply-debates
**Psychological trigger:** Fear (I might be missing an edge)

### H6 — The Process Reveal
**Trigger:** Behind-the-scenes access → dwell time + trust
**Template:** `Here's the exact [process/setup/workflow] I use for [specific outcome].`
**Example:** "Here's the exact checklist I run before entering any DLMM position. Takes 4 minutes."
**Best for:** Threads (step-by-step)
**Engagement target:** Bookmarks + saves
**Psychological trigger:** Desire (I want that process)

### H7 — The Honest Pivot
**Trigger:** Changed mind + intellectual honesty → respect + engagement
**Template:** `I used to believe [X]. I was wrong. Here's what changed my mind.`
**Example:** "I used to think stablecoin LPs were boring. Then I ran the numbers against my directional trades."
**Best for:** Single tweets or short threads
**Engagement target:** Replies (agreement/disagreement split)
**Psychological trigger:** Confusion (why would they change their mind?)

### H8 — The Question-as-Hook
**Trigger:** Direct challenge to reader → ego activation → reply
**Template:** `[Provocative question that assumes suboptimal behavior]?`
**Example:** "If your LP strategy can't survive a 15% drawdown, do you actually have a strategy?"
**Best for:** Single tweets, reply-maximizers
**Engagement target:** Replies and quote tweets
**Psychological trigger:** Fear (am I doing this wrong?)

## Hook Selection Logic

| Topic Type | Primary Hook | Backup Hook |
|---|---|---|
| Sharing a loss or mistake | H1 Confession | H7 Honest Pivot |
| Challenging popular belief | H2 Counterintuitive | H8 Question |
| Breaking news / fresh data | H3 Timestamp | H5 Ignoring This |
| Sharing real results | H4 Builder's Receipts | H1 Confession |
| Underrated mechanic | H5 Ignoring This | H2 Counterintuitive |
| Step-by-step guide | H6 Process Reveal | H4 Receipts |
| Changed opinion | H7 Honest Pivot | H2 Counterintuitive |
| Sparking debate | H8 Question | H2 Counterintuitive |

## Anti-Patterns (hooks that fail for this account)

- "🚨 ALPHA:" — cringe, overused
- "THREAD:" — unnecessary, the 🧵 emoji is sufficient
- "GM everyone" — zero tension, zero curiosity
- "Here are my thoughts on X" — no hook at all, dead on arrival
- "I'm about to share something most people don't know" — guru energy
- Generic questions: "What do you think about SOL?"
