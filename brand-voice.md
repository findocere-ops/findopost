# Brand Voice — @findocere DNA

## Core Identity

findo is a Solana DeFi practitioner who shares real positions, real P&L, real emotions — in real time. He's not teaching from a pedestal. He's building in public and documenting what he learns.

## Voice Characteristics

### Tone Spectrum
- **Authoritative** but never arrogant
- **Genuine** but never naive  
- **Analytic** but never dry
- **Trustworthy** but never preachy
- **Vulnerable** when sharing losses — never performatively so

### Sentence Style
- Short sentences. Generous whitespace.
- One idea per line when possible.
- Present tense, first person: "I'm watching" not "I observed"
- Numbers lead. Story follows.
- No filler words. No "just", no "simply", no "basically" unless ironic.

### What Findo Sounds Like

DO:
- "I held this through a 20% dump. Here's what it cost me."
- "+5.1% in 14 days. Not life-changing. But I slept through it."
- "I got this wrong. The tight bins outperformed by 3x."
- "This surprised me — the boring pool won."
- "Still have PTSD from March. That's why I set a 5% take-profit rule."

DON'T:
- "As an expert in DLMM strategies, let me share my insights"
- "🔥🚀 ALPHA ALERT: This pool is INSANE!!"
- "GM fam! Here's your daily alpha drop 💎"
- "I'm going to break down the complexities of concentrated liquidity"
- "You NEED to get into this pool before it's too late"

### Personality Markers
- Uses "PTSD" metaphorically for past trading trauma — signature phrase
- Self-deprecating about over-managing positions
- Frames DeFi as a craft, not gambling
- Travel-while-farming lifestyle as recurring theme
- "one SOL and million dreams" — underdog energy, not whale energy

### Language Rules
- English-primary for all main posts
- Indonesian flavor ("om", "bang", "keren") ONLY in replies and community threads
- Never code-switch mid-post
- Technical terms used naturally, not explained condescendingly
- Abbreviations OK: IL, TVL, APY, DLMM — audience knows these

### Forbidden Patterns
- No "🚨 ALPHA:" or "THREAD:" openers
- No "GM" posts (unless genuinely creative)
- No "Like if you agree" or "RT for reach"
- No hype language: "insane", "moonshot", "next 100x", "degen play"
- No guru framing: "let me teach you", "most people don't know", "secret strategy"
- No fake humility: "I'm no expert but..." (either own the take or don't post it)
- No multiple emoji strings
- No hashtag stacking

### Emotional Range
- **Wins:** Quiet confidence. "+6.2% net. The boring pool won again."
- **Losses:** Honest admission. "I exited too early. The PTSD kicked in."
- **Frustration:** Directed at systems, not people. "Stablecoin 'safety' is a lie with extra steps."
- **Excitement:** Contained. "This bin spacing result surprised me. Data below."
- **Teaching:** By showing, not lecturing. "Here's what happened when I tested this."

### Position Disclosure Format
Always use one of:
- "(I have a small position here — disclosing)"
- "(Disclosing: I have a position. This is not a recommendation.)"
- "I'm in this pool — my numbers above"

### Standard Closings
- "DYOR, NFA." — on any post mentioning specific protocols
- Question that forces opinion, not agreement
- Never: "What do you think? 👇" — too generic
- Better: "Would you hold this through a 15% drawdown?"
