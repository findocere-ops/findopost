# CTA Library — @findocere

## Reference: [[voice/brand-voice]] for tone. [[platforms/x]] for format.

## Reply-Baiting CTAs (harvest opinions, disagreements, confessions)

- **R1:** "What's your actual number? Drop your real APY — not the advertised one."
- **R2:** "Disagree? Tell me where the logic breaks. I want to stress-test this."
- **R3:** "Be honest — have you ever held a position past your own stop-loss just because the APY looked good?"
- **R4:** "What's the one DeFi mechanic you still don't fully understand? No judgment."
- **R5:** "Would you take this trade with your own money right now? Yes or no — and why."

## Save-Baiting CTAs (make the reader bookmark for later)

- **S1:** "Save this if you LP on Solana. You'll want the checklist when the next volatile week hits."
- **S2:** "Bookmark the thread. When you're setting up your next position, come back to step 3."
- **S3:** "This took me 6 months to figure out. Save yourself the tuition — save this."

## Quote-Tweet-Baiting CTAs (trigger public reactions from builders)

- **Q1:** "Builders: if your protocol's fee structure doesn't survive this scenario, tag me — I want to see the counterargument."
- **Q2:** "If you're working on governance tooling and this doesn't match your model, show me what I'm missing."

## CTA Selection Logic

| Goal | Use | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Maximize replies | R1, R3, R5 | Forces personal disclosure |
| Spark debate | R2, Q1 | Invites disagreement |
| Maximize bookmarks | S1, S2, S3 | Signals future utility |
| Get attention from builders/protocols | Q1, Q2 | Challenges them publicly |
| Build community (newcomers) | R4 | Low barrier, no judgment |

## Anti-Patterns

- NEVER: "Like if you agree" / "RT for reach" / "Follow for more alpha"
- NEVER: "What do you think? 👇" — too generic, zero tension
- NEVER: "Smash that like button" energy
- NEVER: Questions with obvious answers
