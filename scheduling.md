# Scheduling Rules — @findocere Content Calendar

## Posting Windows (UTC)

- **Window 1:** 8:00-10:00 AM UTC (catches US East morning + Europe afternoon + Asia evening)
- **Window 2:** 6:00-9:00 PM UTC (catches US West afternoon + Europe evening)
- **Best single slot:** 8:00 AM UTC (if posting once/day)

## Weekly Rhythm

| Day | Post Type | Formula | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Hack Drop or Confession | F4 or F1 | Start week with quick value or personal reflection |
| Tuesday | Technical Teardown | F3 | Deep thread day — highest effort post of the week |
| Wednesday | Counterintuitive Take or Hack | F2 or F4 | Mid-week engagement driver |
| Thursday | Builder Log | F5 | MeteorWatcher update or AI build progress |
| Friday | Benchmark or Confession | F7 or F1 | End-of-week position update |
| Saturday | Light — reply fuel day | — | Focus on replying to 5+ accounts, no mandatory post |
| Sunday | Weekly Yield Watchlist | F7 | Recurring series — pin after posting |

## Monthly Anchors

- **Week 1:** Establish/re-establish voice. Confession + Hack Drops.
- **Week 2:** Go deep on one pillar. Technical Teardown + Conviction Stack.
- **Week 3:** Cross-pillar synthesis. AI x DeFi, Futarchy x Payments.
- **Week 4:** Community pull. Reply-maximizing posts, polls, audience questions.

## Anchor Post Schedule

1 anchor post per week. These are high-effort, designed to be the week's most-shared piece:
- Anchor = thread with 5+ tweets, screenshots, data, strong hook, bookmarkable
- Pin the best-performing anchor of the month

## Reply Fuel Targets (daily)

Reply to 3+ accounts per day from these categories:
- Meteora ecosystem accounts
- Solana DeFi yield farmers
- AI agent builders
- MetaDAO / governance accounts
- SuperteamINDO community

Rules for replies:
- Add genuine insight, not "great post!"
- Ask a follow-up question when possible
- Show your own data/experience when relevant
- Never self-promote in replies — let the value speak

## Content Pillar Distribution (per 30 days)

- DeFi: ~50% (15 posts)
- Agentic AI: ~20% (6 posts)
- Payments: ~15% (4-5 posts)
- Futarchy: ~15% (4-5 posts)

Never post 2 consecutive posts from the same formula. Alternate modes.
