# X (Twitter) Platform Playbook — @findocere

## Platform Rules

- **Character limit:** 280 per tweet (Premium allows longer, but keep main posts under 280 for reach)
- **Thread limit:** 4-7 tweets ideal. Never exceed 8.
- **Images:** 1-3 per post. Screenshots of real positions perform best.
- **Links:** NEVER in post body. Always in first self-reply. Links kill algorithmic reach.
- **Hashtags:** 0-1 maximum. Prefer zero. One event tag OK (e.g., #SuperteamINDO).
- **Tagging:** Tag max 2 accounts per post. Only tag relevant builders/protocols.
- **Verified:** Blue checkmark (Premium) — unlocks longer posts, but restraint is better.

## Algorithm Optimization Signals (ranked by weight)

1. **Replies** — most valuable signal. Design posts to harvest replies.
2. **Bookmarks** — second most valuable. Dense, actionable content gets saved.
3. **Quote tweets** — third. Contrarian takes and data drops trigger QTs.
4. **Retweets** — useful but less weighted than replies.
5. **Likes** — least algorithmic value, but social proof matters.

### Algorithm Survival Rules
- Early engagement velocity matters — first 30-60 minutes determine reach
- Self-reply with additional context immediately after posting (adds engagement signal)
- Reply to every comment within 1 hour with a follow-up question
- Native images/screenshots boost reach vs. link previews
- Posting at right times: 8-10am UTC and 6-9pm UTC (Solana audience skews US East + Europe + Asia)

## Posting Cadence

- **Minimum:** 1 post per day
- **Optimal:** 1-2 posts per day (never more — quality over quantity)
- **Threads:** 1-2 per week (Tuesday and/or weekend)
- **Reply fuel:** Reply to 3+ credible accounts per day (separate from own posts)

## Post Formats

### Single Tweet (ST)
- Max 280 characters
- Hook + one insight + one question
- Best for: Hack Drops (F4), Counterintuitive Takes (F2), Questions (H8)

### Thread (TH)
- 4-7 tweets
- Tweet 1: Hook + 🧵 emoji
- Tweets 2-5: Numbered steps, data, screenshots
- Final tweet: Takeaway + CTA + DYOR
- Best for: Teardowns (F3), Benchmark Posts (F7), Conviction Stacks (F6)

### Journal Post (JN)
- 1 image (real PNL screenshot) + extended caption
- Confession structure: what happened → how I felt → what I learned
- Best for: Practitioner Confessions (F1)

## Self-Reply Strategy

After every post, immediately self-reply with:
1. Any relevant links
2. Position disclosure if not in main post
3. Additional context or data
4. A follow-up question to seed the reply thread
