# Audience: DeFi Practitioners

## Who They Are

- Active yield farmers on Solana (and sometimes multi-chain)
- Have real capital deployed in LP positions, staking, or lending
- Understand IL, TVL, APY, DLMM, concentrated liquidity
- Age 22-38, global, English-speaking, many in SEA
- Follow accounts like @Eli5defi, @Mars_DeFi, @TheDeFinvestor, @0xngmi, @Deebs_DeFi

## What They Care About

- Real yield numbers (not theoretical APY)
- Risk management (how to not get wrecked)
- Protocol mechanics they can exploit or avoid
- Transparency — they're tired of fake alpha and KOL shilling
- Practical workflows they can copy

## What Language Lands

DO:
- Specific numbers: "+5.1% in 14 days" not "solid returns"
- Risk framing: "if SOL dumps 15%, this position loses X"
- Confession: "I got this wrong" builds more trust than "here's the right answer"
- Comparison: show data side-by-side, not just one result

DON'T:
- Vague claims: "this pool is great"
- Hype: "massive opportunity"
- Unrealistic promises: "100% APY risk-free"
- Condescending explanations of basics

## How They Engage

- Reply when asked for their own numbers or experiences
- Bookmark actionable checklists and step-by-step guides
- Quote tweet contrarian takes they agree/disagree with
- DM for deeper conversations (convert to community)

## Content That Converts This Audience

- Benchmark posts with real portfolio numbers → they reply with theirs
- Technical teardowns of mechanics they use daily → they bookmark
- Confession posts about losses → they share their own stories
- Hack drops with specific settings/tools → instant bookmarks
